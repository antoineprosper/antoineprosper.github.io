I am a cognitive neuroscientist studying visual plasticity in the adult brain. My research combines psychophysics and neuroimaging to understand how our visual system adapts and learns. With a background in biomedical engineering, I am interested in bridging fundamental research with practical applications in neuroscience.

Research Interests

My research focuses on visual plasticity and neural adaptation in the adult brain. I have a particular interest in combining imaging data with computational neuroscience approaches and machine learning techniques to advance our understanding of neural mechanisms in humans.
